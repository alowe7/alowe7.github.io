go down to the river and pray

I remember the first time I heard Allison Krause go down to the river and pray
Anita and I were doing one of our favorite things together going to the garden center on a fine Sunday morning in spring
We were in the car about to park and we both stopped and our jaws dropped
I pulled over and we listened to it and she said something like that makes me feel like a Christian, because I believe in that.

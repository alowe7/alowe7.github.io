The experience

Created At：2024-12-25 18:17 Updated At：2025-03-04 17:20
Keywords: the deep journal

Sometimes I think people who haven’t had or don’t recognize the religious experience are like someone who doesn’t see something so big and so close that it’s all around you. If there is a word for this concept I don’t know what it is

And not just that you haven’t suffered enough.  maybe that too or maybe not, but it hasn’t really rubbed you down to the bone. Stranded you at sea.  Believe me, you'll find God then, my friend.

Maybe you heard about rock bottom or you read it in a book or something but you haven’t really been there. And don’t get me wrong, good for you if so. It’s not like that.

And maybe you even think you have but there’s always something left to lose.

No one feels the wind at their back.

I think the religious experience is like that. Time is so long and there is so much out there we have no idea. No, I mean really. We have no idea. Even the smartest person ever or that ever will be has no idea. Our idea of what might even possibly be possible is not even close.

Reality is inconceivable. And so we use this shorthand for the mysterious ineffable truth and I believe the consciousness and I hope the love, that permeates all reality and is all around you right now, flowing through you, is you, us, we are it.

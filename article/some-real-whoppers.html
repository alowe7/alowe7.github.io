Some real whoppers

I’ve made some mistakes I admit, some real whoppers.

But somehow they brought me here.

Maybe I did it the hard way.

Certainly I did it the hard way.  is there another way?

But after the first one and then the second one and the third one not to mention the fourth one, I couldn’t see another path from where I was at to where I wanted to be.

And it turns out to be ok now, at least compared to what I might have been looking at.

As unlikely as that seemed at the time.

So, thank God for that at least!

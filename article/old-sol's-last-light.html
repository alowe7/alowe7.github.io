Last night I watched the sun go down
And as old sol’s last light became squished and flattened and finally nothing more than a bright yellow dot disappearing behind the horizon I was overcome with a sense of loss and I was reminded of Anita's passing and I longed to have it — the sun — back and to have her — Anita — back with me again

Those days of our youth seemed like the day and these days seem like the night.

The cold, dark, lonely night.

Sometimes it’s unbearable.

Regrets?

Created At：2024-06-10 08:26 Updated At：2026-02-27 16:34
Keywords: journal

is it possible to live a life without regret?

what would that be like?

What is the opposite of regret? Something you’re glad you did or just happened and you would totally do over again the same way? Is there a word for that?

Sometimes I get mired in regret, but then I think of this.

Its had its ups and downs, but at the end of the day, I'm glad I moved to Texas.

I'm glad I met Anita, and marrying her and building a life and a family together with her is probably the defining experience of my life and led to many of the best things that ever happened.  Ever.  At least in my experience.

I'm glad I made the choice to be the one to take care of her, for better or worse.  It wasn't easy, but God's hand was on me.

I'm glad I went to UT, and I'm glad I got into computer science.  I had a decent career, in an interesting time to be in the industry.  Like one colleague of mine said, "you'd have to be an idiot not to get rich in this business."   he was a crook.  but anyway, I met a lot of interesting people like that.  I didn't get rich, I guess, I could have done better, but at the end of the day I guess I did okay.

I could have made some smarter moves in my career, but I guess I did okay.
